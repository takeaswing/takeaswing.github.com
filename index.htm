Awesome Tennis Instruction for Extraordinary Kids.

We love everything about tennis, so we figured: what better way to share our love than to teach it?

That's why in 2007 then-high school sophomore Ari Axler decided to found Take A Swing, a charity dedicated to helping underpriviliged kids learn tennis.

With our fanatical team of USTA-trained volunteers, we host great clinics and tournaments for some really cool kids. We're all high school students, so social situations are our norm.

Where We Came From

Take A Swing Tennis started back in the golden glory days of 2007 when Ariella Axler decided to teach some tennis.

Since then we’ve grown into an awesome organization with tournaments, clinics, and more!

Our board and several volunteers now regularly host instructional sessions for kids around Atlanta.
